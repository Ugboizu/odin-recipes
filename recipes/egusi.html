Egusi Soup Recipe

Egusi Soup is originally from West Africa, it is a popular food found at every occassion.

Ingredients

  • Egusi
  • Vegetable
  • Goat Meat
  • Pepper
  • Red Oil
  • seasoning

Steps

  1. Blend the egusi until smooth and set aside.
  2. Cook your goat meat with the seasonings available. Season with paprika, curry powder, cumin, thyme, pepper, and turmeric; continue cooking until meat is soft.
  3. Remove the already cooked meat from the stock
  4. Add red oil to the stock and other seasonings for soup.
  5. Add the meat and when boiled, add the egusi.
  6. Add the Vegetable and stir.
